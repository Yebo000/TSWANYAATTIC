Who We Are

At Tswanya Attic, we specialize in creating beautifully crafted moulds that bring your ideas to life. Whether you are looking for custom designs, high-quality mould craft for your business, or something unique for your personal projects and desires, we've got you covered.

Founded by Daniella, Tswanya Attic is built on creativity, passion, and dedication to excellence. With years of experience in designing and artistry, we pride ourselves on delivering moulds that are not only functional but also works of art.

We believe every project deserves a strong foundation, and that starts with the perfect mould craft.

Our Mission

To make classic and custom mould designs accessible for homes, studios, and decorators who love sophisticated decor.
